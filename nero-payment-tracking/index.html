What you get

Everything you need to manage payments

Invoice Creation

Issue invoices with immutable on-chain records. Set amount, recipient, due date and description — all stored in the EVM contract.

Payment Marking

Record partial or full payments with timestamps. The contract tracks paid amounts separately from invoice totals for complete audit trails.

Overdue Tracking

Issuers can flag invoices as overdue when due dates pass — keeping all counterparties informed with a single blockchain state change.

Invoice Cancellation

Cancel unpaid invoices with issuer authority. Once cancelled, the state is immutable — protecting both parties from disputes.

Outstanding Balance

Query total outstanding across all active invoices in one call. Excludes paid and cancelled invoices automatically.

Issuer Auth

All write operations require cryptographic signatures. Cancellations and overdue flags are gated to invoice issuers only — no admin keys.

Workflow

From invoice to settlement

1

Connect your MetaMask wallet

Authenticate with your Nero keypair using the MetaMask browser extension. No email or password required.

2

Create an invoice

Fill in the invoice ID, recipient address, amount in Wei, and due date. The transaction is signed and submitted on-chain.

3

Track & manage status

Mark invoices as paid when settlement occurs, flag overdue payments, or cancel if needed. All actions are authenticated.

4

Query your ledger

Look up individual invoices or view total outstanding balances. All data is read directly from contract storage.
